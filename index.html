FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Bacon is a technology platform — your actual investments are held at our licensed brokerage partner, and covered by SIPC insurance up to $500,000. We never touch your money directly.
No minimum. You can start with whatever you're comfortable with — $10, $50, whatever fits your life right now. The most important thing is just getting started.
An ETF (exchange-traded fund) is a basket of stocks or bonds you buy in one shot. Instead of picking individual stocks, you own a tiny slice of hundreds or thousands of companies. It's one of the most diversified, low-cost ways to invest.
Yes — all investing involves risk, including the possible loss of principal. ETF values go up and down with the market. That's why Bacon matches your investment to your timeline — a longer horizon means more time to ride out dips.
A Roth IRA lets your money grow tax-free — you pay taxes now, but never on the gains. A brokerage account is more flexible: no contribution limits, no penalties for withdrawals, but gains are taxed. Bacon helps you figure out which is right for your situation.
